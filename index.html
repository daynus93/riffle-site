Built for the water

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Riffle strips away the noise so logging a catch feels faster than not logging it.

Auto GPS tracking
Start a trip and put your phone away. Riffle records your route in the background while you fish — all day, on one charge.
Quick catch logging
Species, size, lure, depth — logged in seconds. Photo attaches with a tap. One-handed operation designed for cold, wet hands.
Pattern recognition
Over time, Riffle surfaces what's actually working — which lures, which pools, which conditions produce fish for you specifically.
Offline-first
No signal? No problem. Riffle works completely offline and syncs when you're back in range. Built for remote rivers and mountain streams.
Full trip history
Every route, every catch, every photo — stored forever. Search by species, location, or date. Your log book, supercharged.
Map every spot
See every catch pinned to the exact pool or riffle where it happened. Finally answer "where exactly was that spot?" with certainty.
How it works

Three taps. Then fish.

No setup. No forms. No friction between you and the water.

01
Start your trip
Tap once. Riffle begins tracking your GPS route in the background. Lock your phone and get fishing.
02
Log catches as they happen
Species, size, lure — log it in seconds with one hand. Snap a photo or don't. Either way it's captured.
03
Review at the truck
End the trip. See your full route, every catch mapped to where it happened, and your photos in order.
