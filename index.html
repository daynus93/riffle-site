Built for the water

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Riffle strips away the noise so logging a catch feels faster than not logging it.

Background GPS tracking
Start a trip, lock your phone, and fish. Riffle records your exact route in the background all day. Every pool, every run, every wade, mapped without touching your phone.
Two-tap catch logging
Species chip, then checkmark. Done. Designed for cold, wet hands. One-handed, no typing required. Add size, lure, photo, and depth when you want, skip it when you don't.
Fish-dex
Track 186 species across 9 groups. See what you've caught, what you haven't, and your personal best for each one. Your logbook becomes a living record of every species you've ever landed.
Milestones and personal bests
First of a species. Personal best. Biggest fish in a new state. Riffle calls them out automatically so nothing slips by uncelebrated.
Live lock screen widget
During an active trip, your lock screen shows your catch count, distance, and time on the water. Check your stats without ever opening the app.
Works without signal
Built for remote rivers and mountain streams. Riffle saves everything locally and syncs when you're back in range. No cell service, no problem.
How it works

Three taps. Then fish.

No setup. No manual entry. No friction between you and the water.

01
Start your trip
One tap and Riffle starts recording your GPS route in the background. Lock your screen and go fish. It runs all day without draining your battery.
02
Log catches as they happen
Tap a species chip, hit the checkmark. Done in two taps with one hand. Add size, lure, depth, or a photo if you want, or keep moving.
03
Review at the truck
End the trip and see your full route, every catch pinned to where it happened, your stats, and your photos. All in one place.
